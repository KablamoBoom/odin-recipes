Pad Thai

Pad hai (Thai stir fry) is a stir-fried rice noodle dish commonly served as a street food in Thailand as part of the country's cuisine. It is typically made with rice noodles, shrimp, peanuts, a scrambled egg, and bean sprouts, among other vegetables. The ingredients are fried in a wok.

Ingredients

  • 12oz rice noodles
  • 1/4cup coconut oil
  • 7 shrimp, shelled and rinsed
  • 1 egg
  • 1tbsp sriracha
  • 1tbsp fish sauce
  • 1tbsp tamarind or lime juice
  • 1/3cup chopped peanuts

Steps

  1. Soak the noodles in warm water for 15 minutes or until firm
  2. Bring wok up to heat, add oil when smoking
  3. Fry shrimp, noodles, sriracha, fish sauce, and fruit juice approx 4 mins, tossing to keep from sticking
  4. Egg can be added at same time as other ingredients, or fried afterwards
  5. Serve with chopped peanuts on top
