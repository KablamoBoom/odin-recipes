Reuben sandwich

The Reuben sandwich is a North American grilled sandwich composed of corned beef, Swiss cheese, sauerkraut, and Thousand Island dressing or Russian dressing, grilled between slices of rye bread. It is associated with kosher-style delicatessens, but is not kosher because it combines meat and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices rye bread
  • Thousand island dressing
  • 3oz sauerkraut (drained)
  • 2 slices Swiss cheese
  • 3 slices corned beef
  • 1 slice butter

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet or panini press, add butter when hot
  2. Lay first slice of cheese on one slice of rye
  3. Spread thousand island dressing atop cheese
  4. Lay all corned beef slices atop the cheese and dressing.
  5. Spread sauerkraut atop corned beef
  6. Lay final cheese slice atop sauerkraut
  7. Close the sandwich
  8. Press or fry both sides of sandwich until cheese melts.
